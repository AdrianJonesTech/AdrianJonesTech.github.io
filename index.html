About Me

I'm Adrian Jones, a software engineer with over 20 years crafting scalable, high-performance systems that just work. From full-stack development to platform engineering, I've led teams and delivered robust solutions—on-premise or cloud—for everything from legislative tools to VA APIs. My focus? Simple, efficient design that prioritizes clarity and reliability, turning complex challenges into streamlined code.

Leading engineering teams has been the heart of my journey—I've sat with developers in those quiet 1:1 moments, unraveling code knots together, and gently nudged teams toward DevOps rhythms with CI/CD pipelines that eased the chaos of deployments. It felt deeply rewarding to build Solr-optimized search that brought clarity to public legislative chaos, or craft journal apps that captured the pulse of sessions without the usual headaches. Lately, automating API workflows has meant catching outages before they sting, and walking a team through a Django upgrade turned what could have been stress into shared triumphs—restoring trust with partners, smoothing paths for users with better UX, and opening doors for talented hires through thoughtful interviews.

When I'm not optimizing pipelines or refining UIs, you'll find me watching sports, exploring the board game hobby, or cheering on the Licking Heights Marching Hornets! I believe great software, like great engineering, is about precision and purpose.
